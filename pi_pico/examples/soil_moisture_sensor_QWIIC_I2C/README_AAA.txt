ABANDONED this sensor after playing with it.
Could not get it to connect on I2C reliably.
Searching online gave "not a good choice, controller chip on the sensor
is old, design is old. Tends to reset easily, takes 30 secs to reset."
It would connect only sporadically, once a minute or less sometimes.
Tried it with 12" of wire between sensor and Pico. Online recommended
half that or less to make it more reliable.
Not worth the trouble.
