--- Calibration   img_2.jpg

Capacitive sensors are not linear. You must determine the specific values for your sensor:

The capacitance of the soil moisture sensor varies depending on the amount 
of moisture present in the soil.
When the soil is dry, the capacitance of the sensor is low, while when the soil
is wet, the capacitance is high.
  Dry Soil/Air: Note the analog Read (A0) value (e.g., 615)
  Wet Soil/Water: Note the analog Read (A0) value (e.g.,272)
Note: Do not submerge the electronic component part; 
only the probe should be in water.
